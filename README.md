Overview
----
This project visualizes one day of ShangHai MTR Line 5. There are Four coordinated views as shown in Figure 1. 

- View-GeoMap shows 17 Metro lines on geomap and each circle denotes one metro running at this moment.
- View-Line5 is the enlarged view from View-GeoMap.
- View-Passenger-Load presents the passenger load on each metro at this moment. X-axis denotes stations of Line 5 from the start to end and Y-axis denotes the passenger load, how many people on the metro.
- The left side on View-Time indicates time in 24-hour and the right side is a line chart. Here, X-axis denotes the time in minutes. For example, 300 means 5:00 am. And Y-axis denotes how many metros on Line 5 at this moment.
